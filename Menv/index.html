2016

The Beginning

It had been my ambition to pursue graduate studies after a few years garnering work experience on the U.S. east coast. The Master of Environment--Environmental Assessment program at Concordia University in Montréal, Québec felt like the right fit: with its ecclectic curriculum split between policy, method, and data analysis, and its hands-on approach assessing human impact on the environment.

Present

How It Works

The MEnv is a professionally-tracked graduate program in which coursework can be completed within two semesters (less than a year), followed by a four-months internship from which an internship report will be produced to synthesize the professional experience with the environmental assessment literature.
